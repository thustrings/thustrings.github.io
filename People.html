Faculty Members

Wei Song
Associate Professor, YMSC, Tsinghua University

Research interests:
String theory, quantum gravity and holography. In particular, I work on holographic dualities for spacetimes which are not asymptotically Anti-de Sitter, including the Kerr/CFT correspondence, and flat holography. My recent work inludes holographic entanglement entropy for non-AdS holography, the Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev models and integrable irrelevant deformations.
